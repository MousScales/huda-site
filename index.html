About the Creator

Meet the young developer behind Huda

Moustapha Gueye

Founder & Developer

As-salāmu ʿalaykum wa raḥmatullāh,

My name is Moustapha, and I am the creator of Hudā. I am 18 years old at the time of release, and I have been Muslim my entire life, alhamdulillāh. Although I was born into Islam, I understand that many people come to Islam later in life, and I have immense respect for those who find their way to the truth.

This app was developed entirely by me. Every part of it — the design, the code, the content — was created with care, intention, and the hope that it could make a difference in someone's life.

Hudā is not just an app; it's a project of the heart. It's my way of giving back and using the skills that Allah has blessed me with to benefit others, in shā' Allāh. I am still learning and growing, and I hope this app reflects that journey.

May Allah guide us all, increase us in knowledge, and make this effort a source of reward.

Thank you for being here. I truly wish you the best in your journey.

Was-salāmu ʿalaykum wa raḥmatullāhi wa barakātuh.

– Moustapha Gueye, Founder
